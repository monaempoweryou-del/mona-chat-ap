## Production Workflow — One Recording Session to 12 Outputs

```
MONDAY: PLAN (15 min)
  └── Claude proposes 3 LinkedIn posts + 1 YouTube topic for the week
  └── Maor approves or redirects (single reply)

TUESDAY: PRODUCE (60 min Maor time OR fully autonomous via HeyGen)
  └── Option A: Maor records 5-8 min video (core asset)
  └── Option B: Claude generates HeyGen avatar video from script
  └── Claude writes 3 LinkedIn post drafts for review

TUESDAY–WEDNESDAY: PROCESS (Claude, autonomous)
  └── Personal Clipper → extracts 3–4 best clips from core video
  └── Higgsfield Reframe → converts to 9:16 for TikTok/Reels/Shorts
  └── Virality Predictor → scores all clips, flag any below 70
  └── Canva MCP → generates 2 static graphics for LinkedIn text posts

WEDNESDAY–THURSDAY: PUBLISH
  └── LinkedIn: Post 1 + Post 2 (text + graphics)
  └── TikTok + Reels: 2 clips published
  └── YouTube: Full video uploaded

FRIDAY: PUBLISH + REVIEW
  └── LinkedIn: Post 3 (video clip)
  └── TikTok + Reels: 2 more clips
  └── YouTube Shorts: 2–3 clips
  └── GBP: Weekly post (Claude generates, Maor reviews)
  └── Claude reports: best performing post of the week, virality scores
```

**Maor's actual time required per week:** ~75 min (30 min recording or review + 15 min approvals + 30 min reactive engagement)
**Claude's autonomous work per week:** ~4 hours of production, scheduling, and reporting

## Distribution Stack — How Content Flows

```
SOURCE (1 piece)
    │
    ├──► YouTube Full Video (5–15 min)
    │         │
    │         ├──► YouTube Shorts × 3 (Personal Clipper)
    │         ├──► TikTok clips × 2–3 (Higgsfield Reframe → 9:16)
    │         └──► Instagram Reels × 2–3 (same clips)
    │
    ├──► LinkedIn Video Post (1 clip, 60–90 sec)
    │
    ├──► LinkedIn Text Posts × 2 (key insights written out, Canva graphic)
    │
    └──► GBP Post (1 sentence + link, weekly)
```

**Total output from 1 recording:** 1 YT + 3 Shorts + 3 TikTok + 3 Reels + 1 LinkedIn video + 2 LinkedIn text + 1 GBP = **14 pieces of content**

**Virality gate:** All video clips scored before publishing. Minimum score: 70. Any clip below 70 is revised or replaced.
