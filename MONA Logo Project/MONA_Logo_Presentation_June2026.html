Concept A · GPT Image 1.5 (OpenAI)

Pure Wordmark

Clean typographic identity. The name carries the brand alone — no icon required. Confident, corporate, timeless.

Concept A
Pure Wordmark
GPT Image 1.5 · OpenAI
The name is the brand.
MONA rendered in strong geometric sans-serif capitals in deep navy blue. "DIGITAL MARKETING" below in lighter weight uppercase with generous tracking. No icon. No decoration. Pure confidence.
Minimal Corporate Timeless Versatile
Strengths
  • Scales perfectly from favicon to billboard
  • Works in any color on any background
  • Easy to reproduce in any medium
  • Professional at first glance
Considerations
  • Less differentiated — many agencies use wordmarks
  • No icon = no standalone symbol for profile photos
  • Relies entirely on font choice for personality
  • May need iconmark companion for social media

Concept B · Nano Banana Pro (Google)

Geometric M + Wordmark

A bold geometric M lettermark paired with the wordmark. The dual-peak M suggests growth, ascent, and forward momentum.

Concept B
Geometric M + Wordmark
Nano Banana Pro · Google
Growth made visible.
A stylized M formed by two sharp upward triangular peaks — evoking mountains, growth charts, and upward trajectory. Paired with "MONA" in bold modern sans-serif and "DIGITAL MARKETING" in light weight. System approach: icon standalone for social, full lockup for print.
Growth Geometric Premium Memorable
Strengths
  • M icon works as standalone profile photo
  • Growth narrative built into the mark
  • Strong at all sizes
  • Premium, agency-caliber feel
Considerations
  • Geometric M is common in tech/marketing
  • Peaks concept shares territory with other brands
  • Needs color lockup guide for variations

Concept C · Nano Banana Pro (Google)

Signal & Reach

The M constructed from digital signal arcs. Tech-forward, dynamic, and directly communicates the digital marketing mission.

Concept C
Signal & Reach
Nano Banana Pro · Google
Digital by design.
Three upward-rising signal bars or Wi-Fi arcs forming the letter M. Navy blue to electric blue gradient. Immediately communicates digital, reach, and connectivity. The most "digital marketing" of all four concepts. Paired with clean geometric wordmark.
Dynamic Tech-Forward Digital Energetic
Strengths
  • Instantly communicates digital marketing
  • Most distinctive of the four directions
  • Gradient adds energy and modernity
  • Great for social media / video
Considerations
  • Gradient complicates single-color print
  • Wi-Fi arc metaphor may date over time
  • Higher complexity = harder at very small sizes
  • More competitive, less ownable over time

Recommendation & Next Steps

Primary: Concept B — Geometric M
The Geometric M + Wordmark delivers the strongest balance of memorability, versatility, and brand story. The dual-peak M directly visualizes growth and ascent — which IS the MONA promise to every client. It provides a standalone icon for social profiles, a full lockup for documents, and a flexible system that grows with the agency.

The GPT Image Concept (Bold Confidence) is recommended as the direction to develop into a refined professional version — it has the highest brand personality score but needs a human designer pass to reach production quality.
