## Strategic Pillars — 90 Days

### Pillar 1: MONA's Own GBP & Local SEO
**Owner:** SEO Lead | **Channels:** Google Search, Google Maps

The single highest-ROI action for any local business. MONA serves local businesses — it must demonstrate that it practices what it preaches.

**Actions:**
- Claim and fully optimize MONA's Google Business Profile (if not done)
- Complete all GBP fields: services, description with keywords, hours, photos, Q&A
- Weekly GBP posts (updates, tips, offers, case study snippets)
- Request reviews from every satisfied client
- Target categories: "Marketing Agency," "Social Media Marketing Service," "SEO Service"

**Target keywords (local):**
- "digital marketing agency Los Angeles"
- "marketing agency for contractors LA"
- "Google Business Profile management Los Angeles"
- "AI marketing agency Los Angeles"
- "social media management Los Angeles small business"

### Pillar 3: LinkedIn Personal Brand (Maor)
**Owner:** Marketing Director + Social Media Manager | **Channels:** LinkedIn

LinkedIn is the primary B2B lead generation channel. The strategy is Maor as a visible expert — not MONA as a faceless company page.

**Content categories (3 posts/week):**
1. **Client Result** — "We increased Renova Builders' Google reviews by 40% in 30 days. Here's exactly what we did." (Proof)
2. **Lesson/Insight** — "Why most contractors in LA are invisible on Google (and the 3-step fix)" (Education)
3. **Behind the Scenes** — "Building a full marketing report using AI in 12 minutes" (AI Power Studio crossover)

**Format mix:**
- 2 posts: text + single image (Canva-generated graphic)
- 1 post: short video (60–90 sec, HeyGen avatar or Maor on camera)

**Growth tactics:**
- Comment on 5 relevant posts daily (visibility + engagement)
- Connect with 10 LA small business owners weekly (targeted outreach)
- Join and engage in 3 LinkedIn groups: LA entrepreneurs, home improvement, local business

**Month 1 baseline:** Establish profile, post 3x/week, build first 100 connections
**Month 2:** Share first client case study, request 3 LinkedIn recommendations
**Month 3:** Begin LinkedIn newsletter (monthly "LA Digital Marketing Insider")

---

### Pillar 4: Short-Form Video (TikTok + Reels + YouTube Shorts)
**Owner:** Creative Director + AI Power Studio Lead | **Channels:** TikTok, Instagram, YouTube Shorts

TikTok's algorithm distributes based on content quality, not follower count. First video can reach 1M. 3–5 posts per week is the 2026 sweet spot.

**Content categories:**
1. **AI Tool Demos** — "Watch me build a marketing report in 4 minutes with AI" (AI Power Studio audience)
2. **Quick Tips** — "The #1 thing LA contractors need to fix on their Google profile" (MONA client audience)
3. **Before/After** — Show client results visually
4. **Trending hooks + MONA spin** — Use trending audio/format but add MONA's unique perspective

**Production system (one core video → multiple outputs):**
- 1x 5–8 min YouTube/LinkedIn video recorded or HeyGen-generated weekly
- → 3–4 clips extracted via Personal Clipper (Higgsfield MCP)
- → Reframe to 9:16 for TikTok/Reels/Shorts (Higgsfield reframe tool)
- Total: 4–5 short-form posts from one source recording

**Virality check:** Run every video through Virality Predictor before posting. Minimum score 70 before publishing.

### Pillar 5: Referrals + Strategic Partnerships
**Owner:** COO | **Channels:** Direct outreach, local networks

Referrals convert at 3–5x the rate of cold leads. LA service business ecosystem is dense with complementary providers who share the same clients.

**Target referral partners:**
- Business lawyers serving contractors and small businesses
- Accountants and bookkeepers serving home service companies
- Commercial real estate agents (clients often need marketing as they grow)
- General contractors who refer to specialty contractors (pool, taxi fleets, etc.)
- Local chambers of commerce: LA Chamber, West LA Chamber, Latino Business Chamber

**Partnership proposal:** Revenue share OR reciprocal referral (2-way, not transactional)

**Month 1:** Identify 10 potential partners. Reach out to 5.
**Month 2:** Formalize 2 partnerships. Create co-branded one-pager.
**Month 3:** Host a joint webinar ("How LA Contractors Can Get 5-Star Reviews and Rank on Google") — cross-promote to both audiences.

**Owner:** AI Power Studio Lead + Marketing Director | **Channels:** YouTube, LinkedIn, Email (future)

**Flagship content series (YouTube + LinkedIn):**

- **"AI Power Hour"** — Bi-weekly 20–30 min YouTube: Tutorial on an AI tool used in MONA's workflow. Demonstrates expertise. Attracts AI Power Studio audience.

**Educational email (Month 2 onward):**
- Weekly email: "MONA Weekly — 3 things we learned this week in AI marketing"
- Build list from LinkedIn + website opt-in
- Goal: 100 subscribers by Day 90

**AI Power Studio collaboration:**
- Every MONA client result becomes AI Power Studio content
- Every AI Power Studio tool demo references MONA's real use cases
- Share audiences: AI creatives who see AI Power Studio → learn MONA serves businesses → refer business owners
