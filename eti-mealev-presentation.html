03 — Design System

Visual Identity

The design system translates the EDNE sister relationship into a specific, buildable visual language rooted in Haifa, Mediterranean materiality, and architectural permanence.

Color Palette

Charcoal #1C1C1C
Limestone #F5F0E8
Brass #C8A96E
Deep Shadow #2C2C2C
Stone #8A8A8A
Sand #E8E2D8
Color logic: Charcoal (#1C1C1C) is the primary dark — same family as EDNE's bold dark aesthetic. Limestone (#F5F0E8) replaces clinical white with warmth rooted in Mediterranean stone. Brass (#C8A96E) is the single accent — structural, warm, never decorative for its own sake. Used for headings on dark backgrounds, borders, and language toggle only.

Hebrew Copy Structure — Proposed Voice

In Hebrew, less is more — even more than in English. The most powerful Hebrew architectural copy uses precise, spare language. Avoid translation tone. Every Hebrew line must be written natively and reviewed by a Hebrew speaker before delivery.

Recommended Theme Approach

Custom block theme (WordPress Full Site Editing) built on a minimal base. Not a pre-made architecture template. The design system in this document is the template — built to spec, not adapted from stock.

HeaderSticky, transparent over hero, solid on scroll. Logo + nav + language toggle.
