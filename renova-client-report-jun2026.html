2 — Tracking & Measurement Improvements

Accurate conversion tracking is the single highest-impact improvement available in this account. When Google's Smart Bidding system receives incorrect signals, it optimizes toward the wrong behavior — in this case, driving maximum page views at minimum cost rather than driving qualified remodeling leads.

What We Found

The account has 4 conversion actions configured. Analysis confirmed that the primary conversion action is an "all pages views" event — meaning every website visit, including bounces, is counted as a conversion. This causes two problems:

  • The Smart Bidding algorithm is trained to maximize page views, not leads
  • Reported conversion numbers (~2,455/month) dramatically overstate actual lead volume
  • Cost-per-conversion metrics are artificially low ($1.91), creating a false sense of efficiency

What We're Fixing

Conversion Action Current Status Action
"All Pages Views" Primary (incorrect) Demote to Secondary — removes from bidding signal
Phone click / call actions Valid lead signal Confirm as Primary, set minimum call duration (60 sec)
"Services Page — Request Quote" Unverified Confirm tag fires on form submit (not page load)
In progress: Conversion tracking changes require action in the Google Ads UI (Tools → Measurement → Conversions). Instructions have been provided. Once complete, the bidding system will begin retraining on real lead signals within 2–4 weeks.

Expected impact: Once page views are removed from the primary conversion signal, Smart Bidding will shift focus toward queries and audiences that produce actual phone calls and form submissions. This is the single change with the greatest potential to improve lead quality without any increase in ad spend.
